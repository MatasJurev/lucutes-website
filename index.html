About Me

3D Modeling, Graphic Designing, VFX & More

Hi, I am Lukas from Lithuania and I’m highly motivated at making digital arts, including 3D modeling, animation, 2D designing, sound/music design, web design and my recent new skill – visual effects.

Currently I am studying at Vytautas Magnus University and I specialize in Multimedia Technologies, hence the skills I have named.

Services

3D Modeling

3D Modeling with Blender became my hobby in 2019 and I mainly do artistic representations of various objects, but I also got a chance to make 3D assets for a video game.

Animation

Along with 3D modeling I learnt to animate basic and some advanced types of animation, including rigging some objects. I applied this knowledge by animating and exporting a video game character into the Unity game engine.

Music & Sound

I learnt basic sound manipulation and music creation in my university, 2022. Check a couple of examples on my Soundcloud profile!

Photoshop

PS plays an important role in my work. I know basic and a couple of advanced techniques in photo editing and I used PS to make a design for this site.

Visual Effects

VFX is a pretty new skill I'm learning but I'm already applying this ability into a video game movie, called Las Ventura. Link to the movie's trailer.

Photography

I take pictures as a hobby, but I upload some that look really good to the internet. I have gained a lot of experience in photography theory and through practice, check my Flickr profile for examples!
